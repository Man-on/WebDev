Meet the Founder

ELEVATE was founded with the belief that business attire should be both stylish and comfortable, empowering professionals to perform at their best.

Inspiration Behind ELEVATE

It all started when our founder, Christian Man-on, noticed a gap in the market—business attire was either too stiff and uncomfortable or too casual for a professional setting. While working long hours in a corporate job, Christian realized that confidence starts with how you dress. With a vision to redefine business fashion, ELEVATE was born—offering premium-quality clothing that blend elegance, comfort, and professionalism.

“A sharp mind deserves a sharp look. When you feel good in what you wear, success follows.” — Christian Man-on, Founder of ELEVATE.
